Puwedown - Free Video Download Bot

A free, open-source bot that allows users to easily download videos from YouTube and TikTok platforms.

Features:
- Download videos from YouTube with high quality options
- Download videos from TikTok without watermarks
- Simple and user-friendly interface
- Fast and reliable downloads
- No registration required
- Completely free to use

Usage:
Simply provide a video URL from YouTube or TikTok, and Puwedown will fetch and prepare the video for download.

Supported Platforms:
- YouTube
- TikTok

License:
Open source and free to use for personal and non-commercial purposes.

For more information and detailed instructions, please visit the project repository.
